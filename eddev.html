My Current Activities in Educational Development

Currently I am at the Ostfalia University (in Wolfenbüttel, close to Braunschweig, Germany). For 9 years I worked on a German government-funded project for improving education at university-level. The project eCULT (eCompetence and Utilities for Learners and Teachers) provided tools for automated formative assessment of programming exercises.
